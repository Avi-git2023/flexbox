LIFE CYCLE OF BUTTERFLY


The Butterfly Egg

The egg stage usually lasts 3-7 days but this can vary quite a bit among different species of butterflies. In fact some species overwinter as eggs and undergo diapause to survive the many months of winter..


Catterpiller

A very tiny caterpillar hatches from the egg. The first meal for most caterpillars is the eggshell. The caterpillar is designed to be an “eating-machine” and before it begins to pupate it will increase its body mass thousands of times.


Pupa

The picture on the above shows a Black Swallowtail caterpillar getting ready to pupate or form a chrysalis. It has made a silk mat and attached itself at the base. The caterpillar also spun a string of silk around its body and attached the strings to the branch to hold the chrysalis in place in the same way that a telephone linesman uses a leather belt to attach to the pole.
